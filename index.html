Ludo (/ˈljuːdoʊ/; from Latin ludo 'I play') is a strategy board game for two to
four players, in which the players race their four tokens from start to finish
according to the rolls of a single die. Like other cross and circle games, Ludo is
a simpler version derived from the Indian game Pachisi.
